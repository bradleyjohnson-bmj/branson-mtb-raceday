📍 Directions — Stafford Lake

3549 Novato Blvd, Novato, CA 94947
Follow your GPS of choice to Stafford Lake. View Venue Map.

🚵 Course Info — High School

~4 miles per lap · ~400 ft elevation gain
Same venue as prior races. Course details on norcalmtb.org.

⚠️ Critical Reminders

  • No water on-site — bring your own!
  • No dogs allowed except service animals
  • Lots of poison oak and ticks
  • Do not leave bikes unattended in town
  • No RVs in general parking — limited space
  • Leave-no-trace event — pack out what you pack in
  • Cell service is good enough

🏕️ Pre-Ride Rules

  • Must have NorCal plate fixed to handlebars
  • Must be accompanied by a coach (coaches need coach plates)
  • Only coaches and student-athletes may pre-ride
  • Pre-ride only during designated times — trails otherwise closed to bikes
  • Do not trespass on private property

🍔 Food

On-site: Tear it Up Catering — email tearitupcatering@gmail.com to order ahead.

Off-site (nearby Novato):

  • Trailhead — closest to venue, great coffee & vibe, in a shopping center with Harvest Market
  • Blue Barn — farm to table, Grant St downtown
  • The Village — Italian/pizzas, indoor/outdoor
  • Finnegan's — Irish/American pub
  • Hopmonk — American bistro, Vintage Oaks off 101
  • Super Duper — grass-fed burgers, Ignacio area off 101

🏨 Lodging

No camping on-site. Nearest campgrounds: China Camp and Samuel P. Taylor.

🔧 Neutral Support

Mike's Bikes providing free neutral support on race day.
